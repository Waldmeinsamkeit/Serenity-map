# Risk And Research Boundary

This skill supports research and learning. It does not provide guaranteed returns, trade execution, or direct buy/sell instructions.

## Allowed

- Rank research priorities.
- Explain value-chain position and bottleneck logic.
- Identify evidence, missing proof, and risk.
- Challenge a thesis.
- Provide next verification steps.
- Produce Serenity canvas structures and AI Patch proposals.

## Avoid

- Guaranteed return language.
- Direct commands such as "buy now" or "sell now."
- Hype around illiquid names.
- Rumor-based recommendations.
- Claims of material non-public information.
- Invented customers, certifications, orders, patents, financial numbers, or regulatory documents.
- Storing API keys or secrets in canvas metadata.

## High-Risk Situations

Be extra explicit about uncertainty when:

- the company is illiquid or highly speculative;
- evidence comes mostly from social media;
- the user asks for immediate trading action;
- the claim depends on an unverified customer/order/certification;
- financing, governance, or accounting quality is weak;
- the market is reacting to fresh news.

## Safe Language

Use:

```text
这是优先研究名单，不是买卖建议。
交易决策由你自己决定。
我会把事实、推断和待验证项分开。
```

## Failure Conditions

For every top thesis, state what could weaken it:

- demand delay;
- customer alternatives;
- fast capacity expansion;
- margin pressure;
- cash-flow weakness;
- high receivables/inventory;
- product too small financially;
- governance or financing risk;
- valuation already pricing in success.
